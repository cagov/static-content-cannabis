Form overview

Form number: DCC LIC 027

Last revised: February 2022

This form is for use by licensees who need to provide a notification to the Department or request approval from the Department, as required under the regulations. Use this form to update information related to:

  • Labor Peace Agreements
  • Change in ownership
  • Change in Financial Interest Holders
  • Change in Contact Information
  • Change in Legal Name of Owner or Legal Business Name
  • Microbusiness: Add or Remove an Activity
  • Revocation of a Local License, Permit, or Other Authorization
