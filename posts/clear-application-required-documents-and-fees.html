This section covers required documents that must be submitted with your application and licensing fee determination.

Required Documents

The next section requires you to upload a set of documents to support your application. The list of required documents may vary depending on the license type you are applying for. Additional documents may be required depending on how you filled out the application.

Click on the Add button for each document in the list. Upload all the required documents and click Continue when done.

Licensing Fee Determination

The next step is to determine the appropriate license fee due. Estimate the gross revenue for the 12-month license period of the license from the drop-down menu. Your response to this question is used to determine the amount of your license fee. The license fee payment must be received by the DCC before your license will be issued. Click Continue.
