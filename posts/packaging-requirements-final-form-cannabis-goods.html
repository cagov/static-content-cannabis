The Medicinal and Adult-Use Cannabis Regulation and Safety Act (MAUCRSA) includes basic requirements for how cannabis goods must be packaged before retail sale. These guidelines apply to all nonmanufactured and manufactured cannabis goods that will be sold at a licensed retailer. A complete list of requirements can be found in the Department of Cannabis Control’s regulations. (Cal. Code Regs., tit. 4, Div. 19)
