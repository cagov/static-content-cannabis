Keeping cannabis products safe protects consumers and builds trust in the industry. California law sets clear rules for how products must be advertised, packaged, labeled, and sold. These requirements help prevent contamination, ensure accurate information, and reduce risks to public health.

On this page you’ll find resources to guide you through key safety areas—including allergen control, advertising and marketing, packaging, labeling, recalls, and responsible use.

  • Allergens and cannabis safety

    Cannabis products can sometimes contain common food allergens, which may pose health risks for sensitive individuals. To help protect consumers, licensees are expected to understand how allergens may appear in cannabis products and to establish effective control programs.
