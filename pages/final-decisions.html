Decisions adopted pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act 

In certain circumstances, a license denial, citation or disciplinary action will result in DCC issuing a final Order of Decision pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act. The following table provides information related to those decisions and allows you to view the Order of Decision by clicking in the case documents column.  

Final Decisions are posted on the Department of Cannabis Control’s website for one year. If you would like to obtain a Final Decision older than a year, please send a Public Records Act request to publicrecords@cannabis.ca.gov.

If you require an ADA compliant version of any of the above Order of Decision documents, please send a request to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov
