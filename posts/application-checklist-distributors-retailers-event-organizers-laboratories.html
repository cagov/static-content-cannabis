Distributors


Have the following documents ready in case they are requested during the application review process

  • Standard operating procedures (Form DCC-LIC-019)
  • Proof of insurance as defined in section 15308
  • Transport vehicle information as defined by section 15312

Retailers

Have the following documents ready in case they are requested during the application review process:

  • Standard operating procedures (Form DCC-LIC-019)
  • Delivery employee information as defined by section 15415
  • Delivery vehicle information as defined by section 15417

Cannabis event organizers

Input the following information:

  • Number of temporary events the applicant will hold during the 12-month license period

Note: A cannabis event organizer license shall not be required to comply with subsections (c)(5), (c)(17), (c)(20), (c)(21), (c)(22), (c)(24), (c)(25), and (c)(26) of 15002.

Testing laboratories:

Upload the following documents:

  • Certificate(s) of accreditation as defined by sections 15701 and 15702, or the information required for an interim license as defined by section 15703.
  • Method validation reports for specified testing methods
  • Standard operating procedures (Form DCC-LIC-019)
