Dear California Community Reinvestment Grants Program Interested Parties:

The Governor’s Office of Business and Economic Development (GO-Biz) is excited to announce that the California Community Reinvestment Grants (CalCRG) program Grant Solicitation is now available!

The Grant Solicitation and link to the Online Application Portal, as well as an Application User Guide and Frequently Asked Questions, can be found on the CalCRG website at www.business.ca.gov/CalCRG.  Click on the ‘Apply Now’ button located on the CalCRG website to access the Online Application Portal.

GO-Biz will hold a Technical Assistance webinar to assist applicants with questions about the application process and program requirements.  The live webinar will be held on Tuesday, April 30, 2019 from 1:00-3:00pm.  Interested parties can register for the webinar here.

Applications must be submitted no later 11:59pm on Thursday, May 16, 2019.  GO-Biz will review applications and award grants May 17, 2019 through July 31, 2019.  The anticipated grant term will be August 1, 2019 through July 31, 2021.

If you have any questions or comments, you may email us at CalCRG@gobiz.ca.gov or calling us at 916-322-2683 to speak to a CalCRG staff member.

Thank you for your time and we look forward to reviewing your application!
