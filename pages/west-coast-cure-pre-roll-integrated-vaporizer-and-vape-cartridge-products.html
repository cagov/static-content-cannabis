Recall overview

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is issuing this notice of voluntary recall for multiple products packed and manufactured by Shield Management Group, LLC and Alkhemist DM, LLC, due to potential adulteration. This recall includes products packaged under the following brand names:

  • “West Coast Cure CUREjoint” pre-roll
  • “West Coast Cure Disposable CUREpen Premium THC Oil” integrated vaporizer
  • “West Coast Cure CUREpen Premium THC Oil” vape cartridge
  • “West Coast Cure CUREpen” vape cartridge
  • “West Coast Cure Live Resin” vape cartridge

Product details

This recall applies to the following “West Coast Cure” pre-roll, integrated vaporizer, and vape cartridge product[s].

What consumers need to know

If you purchased this product:

  • If you are experiencing symptoms or any adverse reactions, contact your physician immediately.
  • Check your package for the UID and batch number(s) above.
  • If the numbers match, dispose of the product or return it to the retailer for proper disposal.
  • Cannabis vape cartridges and cannabis integrated vaporizers must be properly disposed of as hazardous waste at a household hazardous waste facility or other approved facility.

What licensees need to know

DCC sent notices to all licensees with this product in their inventory with instruction about responding to this recall. Contact our team at recalls@cannabis.ca.gov if you have any questions.
