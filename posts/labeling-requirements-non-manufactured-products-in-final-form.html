Cannabis must be properly labeled to make sure consumers are informed about what they are buying and to prevent unintended use. These guidelines cover labeling requirements for non-manufactured cannabis goods that are ready to be sold by a licensed retailer. The checklist below will help you verify that your labeling complies with Department requirements. A complete list of requirements can be found in the Department of Cannabis Control’s regulations. (Cal. Code Regs., tit. 4, Div. 19)

Included in this checklist

  • Labeling placement
  • Labeling dos and don’ts
  • Labeling checklist (for outer layer of packaging)
