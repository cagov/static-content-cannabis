The Department of Justice filed on behalf of the Department of Cannabis Control’s (DCC) an action to adopt a revised version of the cannabis licensee bond form. The revised form replaces the version used by the three legacy cannabis agencies. Revisions to the form replaced reference to any of the former three licensing authorities with “Department of Cannabis Control”. OAL approved and filed the revised form with the Secretary of State on December 7, 2023, making it effective the same day.
