The California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA) has adopted proposed emergency Cannabis Tax Regulation 3702, California Cannabis Track-and-Trace, to require the wholesale cost and retail selling price of cannabis and cannabis products to be recorded in the California Cannabis Track-and-Trace (CCTT) system. 

To view the notice of proposed emergency action, please click on the following link:  http://www.cdtfa.ca.gov/taxes-and-fees/reg-3702-2018.htm. 

After submission of the proposed emergency regulation to the Office of Administrative Law (OAL), OAL will allow interested persons five calendar days to submit comments on the proposed emergency regulation.  More information about OAL’s notice and comment process is available on OAL’s website at https://oal.ca.gov/regulations/emergency_regulations/Emergency_Regulation_Process/.
