When you begin the renewal process, you will be asked to verify the list of license owners and financial interest holders.

Owners Verification

To begin, verify that the List of Owners and Entity Ownership is current. If the list of owners is not accurate, the licensee must report the ownership updates using form DCC LIC 027 and submit to licensechange@cannabis.ca.gov.

Please move forward with the renewal after the form has been submitted. Then attest to the Ownership Verification by checking the box, and click “Continue.”

Financial Interest Holders

Verify Financial Interest Non-Owners and the Entity Financial Interests is current. If the list of Financial Interest Holders is not accurate, the licensee must report the changes using form DCC LIC 027 and submit to licensechange@cannabis.ca.gov.

Please move forward with the renewal after the form has been submitted. Then attest to the Financial Interest Verification and click Continue.
