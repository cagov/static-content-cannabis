Selecting this option allows the cultivator to permanently modify the renewal date one time to provide more flexibility for when fees are due.

Definition

  • “Modified License Term” means-
    • A timeframe that is less than 12 months; and
    • Falls between the original and the modified date of renewal. 

Eligibility

  • All cultivation license types, including Nursery and Processor, are allowed to modify their renewal date one time.
  • Modification of the renewal date is only allowed once per cultivation license and becomes permanent once the renewal is paid and approved.

Fees

  • Cultivators will receive a prorated annual license fee for the Modified License Term.
    • The prorated fee is calculated by dividing the applicable annual license fee by 365 and multiplying that value by the number of days between the date the original and modified renewal date.

Formula-

(Existing Annual Fee / 365)  x Number of Days = New Annual Fee

Example-

Small Outdoor license type = $4,820 annual fee

Current Expiration Date = March 1, 2025

Modified Expiration Date = November 1, 2025

Calculation-

($4,820 / 365) x 245 = $3,235.34

Prorated Fee-

New Annual Fee = $3,235.34

Equity Fee Deferrals

If you have obtained an Equity Fee Deferral, you are required to pay all license fees in full within six months of the deferral being granted or prior to the modified renewal date, whichever is sooner.

How to request this option 

  1. Review the regulations and the licensing system renewal guidance document.
  2. Login to your CLS account.
  3. Select the license you want to renew.
  4. Input your desired selections and any requested information.
  5. Upload required documentation, if applicable.
  6. Pay the license fee.
  7. Submit your renewal request. 

Background

The Department has adopted emergency regulations to implement Senate Bill 833. Under these regulations, there are new options available for licensed cultivators renewing their license.

Criteria:

  • Your cultivation license expires on or after March 1, 2024
  • Changes are requested during your renewal period

These opportunities are voluntary and are selected at the discretion of the cultivator.
