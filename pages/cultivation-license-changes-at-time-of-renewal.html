Type:  Emergency rulemaking

Status: Filed with OAL on August 23, 2024 and currently under review. Public comments accepted through August 28, 2024.

Summary: The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) has filed an action with the Office of Administrative Law (OAL) to readopt its emergency regulations implementing Senate Bill 833, codified in Business and Professions Code section 26061.5, which requires the DCC to allow cultivation licensees to make certain changes such as changing the type of size of a cultivation license; placing a cultivation license in inactive status; or making a one-time change to a cultivation license’s date of renewal.

View the proposed finding of emergency and notice of proposed readoption and the proposed text of emergency regulations below:

  • Emergency Regulation Text

Public comments process

The five-calendar day public comment period for this emergency action starts once OAL posts notice of the filing on its website.

View the emergency regulations

OAL is accepting public comments on the proposed emergency action through Wednesday, August 28, 2024.

If you wish to comment on the proposed emergency regulations, you must submit your comment in writing to both OAL and DCC. Verbal comments cannot be accepted.
