The Real CA Cannabis campaign has raised consumer awareness about buying cannabis from legal retailers in California. To help you leverage this, we encourage linking your business to the campaign on social media. This flexible toolkit provides content options—from subtle to direct messaging—so you can align with platform guidelines while maintaining your brand’s voice.

General social media guidance

  • Ensure that your bio reads “Content intended for 21+” or something similar.
  • Ensure that your bio reads “Nothing for sale” or something similar.
  • Update your bio to include a link to the Real search: “Licensed California Retailer. Verify at: https://bit.ly/4geVk4P
  • Use Bitly or your preferred link shortener if possible to avoid having direct cannabis links in your social media posts or bio.

Do’s

  1. Be sensitive: Everyone experiences cannabis differently and utilizes it for various reasons. Please only speak to your own personal experiences using cannabis.
  2. Inform your followers! Share why it is important to purchase cannabis from a licensed, legal retailer—educating consumers is the purpose of Real California Cannabis!
  3. Include a call-to-action: Find legal dispensaries at real.cannabis.ca.gov/licensed-retailers

Don’ts

  1. Don’t include minors (anyone under 21) or pets.
  2. Don’t use language that contains any health-related statement that is untrue in any particular manner or tends to create a misleading impression as to the effects on health of cannabis consumption.
  3. Don’t post content that displays nudity or undergarments.
  4. Don’t use offensive or profane language.
