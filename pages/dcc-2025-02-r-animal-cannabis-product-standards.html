Summary:â€¯ The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is proposing a rulemaking action to establish standards for cannabis products intended for use on, or consumption by, animals.

Specific changes include:

  • Clarifying that animal cannabis products are subject to the same requirements as adult-use cannabis products.
  • Limiting animal cannabis products to edible cannabis products, orally consumed concentrates, and topical cannabis products.
  • Limiting animal cannabis products to no more than 1 milligram of total THC per package.
