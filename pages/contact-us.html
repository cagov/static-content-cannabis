Contacts for other stakeholders

DCC and licensees interact with other state agencies and questions may need to be directed to the appropriate agency.  

Local jurisdictions may have stricter cannabis regulations than that of the state. Certain inquiries may require you to reach out to your local jurisdiction.

Events and press
