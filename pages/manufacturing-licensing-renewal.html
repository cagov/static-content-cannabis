Owners Verification

To begin, verify that the List of Owners and Entity Ownership is current. If the list of owners is not accurate, the licensee must report the ownership updates using form DCC LIC 027 and submit to licensechange@cannabis.ca.gov.

Please move forward with the renewal after the form has been submitted. Then attest to the Ownership Verification by checking the box, and click “Continue.”

Financial Interest Holders

Verify Financial Interest Non-Owners and the Entity Financial Interests is current. If the list of Financial Interest Holders is not accurate, the licensee must report the changes using form DCC LIC 027 and submit to licensechange@cannabis.ca.gov.

Please move forward with the renewal after the form has been submitted. Then attest to the Financial Interest Verification and click Continue.

Labor Peace Agreement (LPA)

Select your answer to the questions below regarding number of employees. Depending on what you select, you will need to provide either a form DCC 9205 labor peace agreement notarized statement or a copy of the signature page of your LPA when you reach the document upload screen. In addition, you will need to provide the Labor Organization name and the LPA expiration date. Click “Continue.”

Declarations

Select “Yes” or “No” in the Declarations section then click “Continue.” If you select “No” to the under penalty of perjury section, an error message will display, and you will not be able to complete the renewal. Please email Licensing@cannabis.ca.gov for assistance.

Note: if you selected ‘Yes’ to making changes to your operational activities under Renewal Process, it will take you to a page where you will need to select the new Operational Activities. See an example in the visual.

Documents

The next section requires you to upload documents to support your total gross revenue for the current license period and Labor Peace Agreement requirement. Click on the “Add” button for each document in the list. See visual below.

Examples of acceptable gross revenue documentation include, but is not limited to, a copy of the licensee’s state tax return or a detailed Profit and Loss statement (P&L) showing gross revenue for the current license period.

Upload all the required documents and click “Continue.”

Renewal Fees

In the Renewal Fees section select the gross revenue amount from the preceding 12 months. Please read the Important Notice section then click “Continue.”

Review

Please review all the information to be correct. Click on the “Edit” button to make any changes.

Read the Important Notice section (at the very bottom) then click Continue.

Payment

The next step is to pay the application fee for the license. You can pay online by credit or debit card. You also have the option to mail in a physical check to our address. Additionally, you can schedule an appointment to pay in person at one of our three offices by cash. For more information on payments visit DCC website or email us at payments@cannabis.ca.gov for questions.

Note: If requesting a fee deferral, select to pay with Cash. Email equity@cannabis.ca.gov after renewal has been submitted to discuss fee deferrals, or for general customer service email licensing@cannabis.ca.gov.

If you choose the options for “Cash or Mail-In Check,” the system displays a confirmation message and sends you an email reminding you to pay the fee.

If you choose the option for Credit/Debit Card, the “Pay Now” page shows you the application number and fee you’re about to pay. Click “Checkout” to continue.

You will be redirected to a third-party page. Enter your billing address and payment Information and click “Submit.”

You will be redirected to a third-party page. Enter your billing address and payment Information and click “Submit.”

Note: A 2.99% fee is charged by the payment processor for credit or debit card transactions.

A confirmation message will display when payment has been processed.

After making your payment, if there are no outstanding issues, the license will show a new “Expires On” date reflecting that your license has been renewed for one more year.

In your portal, click on “My Records” and locate your license number. Click on the “License Number” link to open your license record and print your license.

In your license record, click on the “Record Info” dropdown list and select “Attachments.”

At the top of your attachments list, you should see your official license certificate. You can open this PDF by clicking on its title.

Print your new license certificate and post it in a visible space where visitors to your facility can see it.
