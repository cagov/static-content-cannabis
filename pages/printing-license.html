Finalizing and printing your license is simple with this guide. Once your application is approved and the license fee is paid, you’ll be able to access, view, and print your official license certificate.

Start by paying the required license fee, as outlined in the previous guide. Once payment is complete, your license will be activated and valid for one year. You can then access your license record, view any attachments, and download your official certificate. Finally, print the certificate and ensure it’s posted at your licensed premises as required by law.

Paying the license fee

If your application is approved, you must pay the license fee before your license can be issued.

On the home screen, Click on the link “Pay Fees Due.”

Refer to the Paying the application fee guide for steps on how to pay. Failure to pay initial licensing fees within 60 days of approval will result in the application being abandoned.

Open your license record

After making your payment the license status will show as Active and will be valid for one year. Click on the License Number link to open your license record and print your license.

View attachments

In your license record, click on the Record Info dropdown list and choose Attachments.

View license certificate

At the top of your attachments list, you should see your official license certificate. You can open this PDF by clicking on its title.

Print your certificate

Print your new license certificate and post it at your licensed premises.
