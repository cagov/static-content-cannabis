How to renew your cannabis cultivation license

  • CLS renewal documents

    You may be be required to upload several documents into the licensing system during the renewal process, including electricity usage reporting documentation, a premises diagram, and documentation related to labor peace agreements.

  • Reporting electricity usage during CLS renewal

    You will need to input electricity usage data through the licensing portal in order to renew your license. Review the different types of data you will need to provide and resources to help you with this step.

  • Verifying business, people, and owners during renewal

    Ensuring accuracy of all owners and other people listed in the CLS portal during renewal is a critical step in the process. Review these steps to verify owners and financial interest holders.
