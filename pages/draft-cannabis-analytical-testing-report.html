All batches of cannabis and cannabis products are sampled for regulatory compliance testing prior to being released to retail. Each sample of cannabis and cannabis products are tested for a variety of analytes as prescribed by Department of Cannabis Control regulations.

This report is a statistical and graphical analysis of all cannabis and cannabis products that have been sampled and tested for regulatory compliance since the inception of the Department of Cannabis Control, July 12, 2021. The report identifies the category of each sample of cannabis or cannabis product, and each analyte that the sample failed testing for.
