Easily access your license record, view attachments, and print your license certificate with this detailed guide. Manage your license documents effectively with our step-by-step instructions.

If your application is approved, you must pay the license fee before your license can be issued.

On the home screen, Click on the link Pay Fees Due.

Refer to the How to Pay Fees guide for steps on how to pay License Fee. Failure to pay initial licensing fees within 60 days of approval will result in the application being abandoned.

Open your license record

After making your payment the license status will show as Active and will be valid for one year. Click on the License Number link to open your license record and print your license.

View attachments

In your license record, click on the Record Info dropdown list and choose Attachments.

View license certificate

At the top of your attachments list, you should see your official license certificate. You can open this PDF by clicking on its title.

Print your certificate

Print your new license certificate and post it at your licensed premises.
