California state law requires many commercial cannabis licensees to enter into, and abide by, labor peace agreements. For more information, please see Labor Peace Agreement for Cannabis Businesses on the Department of Cannabis Control website.

Select your answer to the questions below regarding number of employees.  Depending on what you select, you will need to provide either a form DCC 9205 of notarization, or a copy of the signature page of your LPA when you reach the document upload screen.  In addition, you will need to provide the Labor Organization name and the LPA expiration date.
