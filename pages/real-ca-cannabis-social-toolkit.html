The Real CA Cannabis campaign is widespread, with millions of consumers seeing ads across various platforms about the importance of buying cannabis from legal retailers in California. To help you capitalize on this increased consumer awareness, we encourage you to link your business to the campaign on social media. However, we understand the challenges of posting cannabis-related content due to varying platform guidelines. This flexible toolkit offers a range of content options, from subtle messaging to direct engagement, allowing you to choose the level that best fits your business needs while ensuring compliance with platform rules and maintaining your brand’s voice.

General social media guidance

  • Ensure that your bio reads “Content intended for 21+” or something similar.
  • Ensure that your bio reads “Nothing for sale” or something similar.
  • Update your bio to include a link to the Real search: “Licensed California Retailer. Verify at: https://bit.ly/4dVfRcq
  • Use Bitly or your prefered link shortener if possible to avoid having direct cannabis links in your social media posts or bio. 🌿
