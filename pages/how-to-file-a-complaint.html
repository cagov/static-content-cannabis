Commercial cannabis activity

DCC licenses and regulates all commercial cannabis activity in California. This includes the growing, manufacturing, distribution, testing, and sale of cannabis and cannabis products.  

You can file an anonymous complaint against any cannabis business, licensed or unlicensed.  

Human trafficking

DCC can help route complaints about human trafficking, which is a complex issue that requires the involvement of multiple agencies.
