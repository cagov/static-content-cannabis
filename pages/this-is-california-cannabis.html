This campaign features vibrant and compelling videos, photographs, and other materials that tell the stories of our licensees and explain the benefits of the legal cannabis industry. Learn more about the hard-working licensees driving innovation and creating a safe, sustainable, and equitable cannabis market. 

Interested in using these digital ads, 30-second public-service radio announcements, billboard art, social-media content, or videos? Contact us by sending an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov.

Licensee profiles

At the core of our campaign is the community of dedicated licensees paving the way for generations to come. Do you know outstanding licensed cannabis operators who deserve to be recognized? Are you interested in sharing your story? 

We’d like to feature exceptional stories of cannabis licensees in our newsletter, social media posts, and ad campaigns. Please send an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov with details and a DCC communications team member will respond.
