Type: Regular rulemaking 

Status: Approved and filed December 30, 2022. Effective January 1, 2023.

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) has adopted regulations t for obtaining a Large Cultivation License under MAUCRSA. These regulations also provide a pathway for existing licensees to convert to multiple smaller licenses into a Large or Medium Cultivation License.
