Type: Regular rulemaking 

Status:  Accepting public comments through May 23, 2023

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is providing notice to the public of its intent to adopt regulations to implement and make specific Business and Professions Code section 26100(f)(2) pertaining to the establishment of a standard cannabinoids test method, including standardized operating procedures, that shall be utilized by all licensed testing laboratories in California. Through the proposed regulations, the DCC aims to ensure all licensed laboratories are using the same standardized cannabinoid test method which will ensure consumers receive accurate and consistent information regarding the cannabinoid content of the cannabis and cannabis product they use or consume.

How to submit a public comment

You can comment on the proposed regulations through May 23, 2023. You can submit comments by mail or email. You only need to submit your comment by one method. 

Send your comment to DCC, and reference the rulemaking topic (“Additional Modifications to Test Method and Standardized Operating Procedures Regulations”): 

  • By mail: DCC Legal Affairs Division, 2920 Kilgore Road, Rancho Cordova, CA 95670
  • By email: publiccomment@cannabis.ca.gov
