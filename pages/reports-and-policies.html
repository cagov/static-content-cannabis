Economic reports

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) engaged ERA Economics to prepare a report summarizing current market conditions and outlook for California’s cannabis industry. This report provides a snapshot of industry conditions (i.e., economics of the cannabis market) and recent trends. It provides an overview of factors affecting the supply of cannabis, consumer demand for cannabis, and how these factors are changing over time in California and other markets.
