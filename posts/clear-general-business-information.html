In this first part of the application, you will cover the following sections: General disclaimer, License Designation, Business Activities, Business, Contact/Business Information, Contact Person and Agent for Service of Process, and Declarations.

From the Home screen, choose the type of license you wish to apply for.

There are a total of 9 license types you can choose from.

  • Retailer
  • Retailer Non-Storefront
  • Distributor
  • Distributor-Transport Only
  • Microbusiness
  • Testing Laboratory
  • Manufacturing
  • Shared-Use (note: for manufacturing facilities only)
  • Cannabis Events

General disclaimer

Once you select a license type, you are asked to accept the General Disclaimer before beginning your application. Check the box and select Continue.

License Designation

Next, you will choose between Adult-Use and Medicinal license type designations. You may also select both if permitted by your local jurisdiction.

Business Activities

The Business Activities section may display different information for different license types. The example provided shows confirmation that the Retailer Non-Storefront license type provides delivery service. Other business types may ask you to select various services your operation will provide.

Business

The section Labeled Business is where you will establish the organization structure of your entity and provide the other details about how you do business. All the information on the next screen is required. Click Add New.

Contact/Business Information

A pop-up window will open for you to enter the following information:

  • Business organization structure (choose from drop down list)
  • Name of Business/DBA
  • Email
  • Phone Number
  • FEIN

Next, click Add Address

A new pop-up window displays. You must enter a Premises address and a Mailing address. You can add both addresses by selecting Save and Add Another.

After you have entered both a Premises address and a Mailing address, Click Save and Close.

You will be back on the Contact information pop-up window. Verify all the information is correct. Click Continue to return to the application.

Contact Person and Agent for Service of Process

Next, you will set up a Primary Contact Person for your business. This is the person who will communicate with the Department. If you are the Primary Contact, click Select from account. Or select Add New if the Primary contact is a different individual.

Set up an Agent for Service of Process for your business. The agent of service is an authorized contact. This contact will be able to view the application and any subsequent records it is added to. You can Select from Account or Add New to add a different individual. Click Continue.
