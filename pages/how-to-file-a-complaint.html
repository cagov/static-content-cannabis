Discrimination

What is discrimination?

Discrimination is the act of making distinctions between people based on the groups, classes, or other categories to which they belong or are perceived to belong. People may be discriminated on basis of race, gender, age, religion, disability, or sexual orientation, as well as other categories.

Agency to contact: CA Civil Rights Department
