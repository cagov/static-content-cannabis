The Department of Cannabis Control has two public awareness campaigns to:

  • Educate consumers about buying from licensed retailers
  • Encourage unlicensed businesses to get licensed
  • Promote California’s legal cannabis market

This is California Cannabis

2020-present

This is California Cannabis focuses on the cannabis market. The campaign:

  • Promotes the legal cannabis market
  • Highlights hardworking members of the legal cannabis community
  • Promotes the benefits of a strong and safe legal market

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Email newsletters
  • Videos
Learn more about This is California Cannabis

CannaConnect Compliance Hub

2021-present

CannaConnect connect California’s cannabis license holders to the tools they need to thrive in the state’s legal cannabis market:

  • Applying for a license
  • Transitioning from a provisional to annual license
  • Preparing for an inspection
  • Using Track and Trace
  • Requirements for cannabis goods

The campaign includes:

  • Social media posts
  • Presentations
  • Videos
Learn more about CannaConnect

Get #weedwise

2012-2021

Get #weedwise educates consumers about:

  • The risks associated with illicit products
  • How to identify a licensed retailer
  • It also warns businesses of the consequences of operating without a license.

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Presentations
