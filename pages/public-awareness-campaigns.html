The Department of Cannabis Control has two public awareness campaigns that help:

  • Educate consumers about buying from licensed retailers
  • Encourage unlicensed businesses to get licensed
  • Provide resources for licensed cannabis businesses

This is California Cannabis

2020-present

Using stunning images, video, and compelling stories, this campaign:

  • Promotes the legal cannabis market
  • Highlights hardworking members of the legal cannabis community
  • Promotes the benefits of a strong and safe legal market

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Email newsletters
  • Videos
