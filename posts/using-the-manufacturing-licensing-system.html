How to access

Manufacturers: new users

Click “Manufacturers: new users” to create an account and begin your annual license application.

Manufacturers: returning users

Click “Manufacturers: returning users” if you already have a username, and wish to return to your application or edit your license information.

Tips for using the licensing system

  • Do not use your browser’s back button.
  • When signing your name, select Accept below the signature block before selecting Next.
  • You can only upload files 50 MB or smaller.
