Welcome to the CLEaR System User Guides, your resource for navigating the online licensing and renewal process. Whether you’re applying for or renewing licenses for distributors, testing laboratories, manufacturers, retailers, microbusinesses, or events, these guides provide clear, step-by-step instructions to help you complete each process.

For each license type, you’ll find guidance on registration, account management, application submission, and renewal processes. Simply select the guide that matches your needs and get started.

  • CLEaR: Creating an account

    The first item you’ll need to complete in when applying for a cannabis business license with the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is to register on the website and create your own account. All business owners applying for a cannabis business license must be registered and have an active account with the online licensing system.

  • CLEaR: Account navigation

    Once you are registered and logged into your account, you can begin the process of applying for a cannabis license. This guide provides an overview of selecting a license type and navigating to key sections of the system.
