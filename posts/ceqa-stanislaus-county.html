Background 

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) currently issues commercial cannabis cultivation licenses under Division 10 of the Business and Professions Code and California Code of Regulations, title 4, division 19. All legal commercial cannabis activity must be conducted pursuant to state licensing requirements. DCC acts as Lead Agency for commercial cannabis activities occurring in Stanislaus County that require a state license.
