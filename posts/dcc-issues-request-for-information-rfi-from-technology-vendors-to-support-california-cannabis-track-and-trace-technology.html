The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is seeking information from technology vendors to better understand current software products that are available and can meet the needs of the California Cannabis Track and Trace (CCTT) system. These efforts support the department in making informed decisions about the services it contracts for on behalf of its licensees. 

Collective information may be used to develop alternatives for consideration and estimate costs related to this system. Responses to this request will be performed at no cost to the State. 

The deadline for responses is July 3, 2023.
