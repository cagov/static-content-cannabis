Learn how to calculate the fees associated with certain types of cultivation license changes related to the implementation of Senate Bill 833:  reducing the size of a cultivation license, placing the cultivation license in Limited Operations Status, and making a one-time change to the expiration date of a processor, nursery, or cultivation license. 

Based on the nature of the modifications requested by the licensee, the Department will calculate fees due, or refund owed.

Example scenario

A licensee renewed their processor license on October 1, 2023.  The licensee would prefer to renew their license annually on February 1, and submits a request to make a one-time change the license’s expiration date, on May 1, 2024.   

How a refund is calculated for the original cultivation license 

Calculate the prorated daily license fee by dividing the annual processor license fee by 365 days in a year. 

Calculation formula 

$9,370.00 annual processor license fee, divided by 365 days = $25.67 prorated daily license fee.  

Calculate the number of licensed days remaining in the original license term, by counting the number of days between the date of the request, May 1, 2024, and the original expiration date, October 1, 2024.

Result:  

There are 153 days remaining in the original license term. 

Calculate the amount of credit the license is eligible for by multiplying the number of days remaining in the original license term by the prorated daily license fee. 

Calculation formula  

153 (Step 2) x $25.67 (Step 1) = $3,927.51 credit. 

How fees are calculated for a modified my license term  

Calculate the number of days in the modified license term, by counting the number of days between the date of the request, May 1, 2024, and the new expiration date on February 1, 2025.

There are 276 days in the modified license term. 

Calculate the fee for the modified license term by multiplying the number of days in the modified license term by the prorated daily license fee.  

Calculation formula

276 (Step 4) x $25.67 (Step 1) = $7,084.92 modified license fee. 

Calculate the amount due, by subtracting the credit calculated in Step 3 from the modified license fee calculated in Step 5. 

Calculation formula

$7,084.92 (Step 5) – $3,927.51 (Step 3) = $3,157.56 due to DCC for the modified license term. 

How fees are calculated when a cultivation license changes their expiration date and either requests to place their license on limited operations status or reduce the size of the cultivation license

Example Scenario – A licensee renewed their medium mixed-light tier 1 license on October 1, 2023.  The licensee would prefer to renew their license annually on February 1, and plans to submit a request to make a one-time change to the license’s expiration date, on May 1, 2024.  The cultivation licensee would also like to either place the cultivation license in limited operations status or request a reduced-size cultivation license and decides to calculate both fee options to inform their decision.

To calculate fees due to change of expiration date: 

Calculate the prorated daily license fee by dividing the annual medium mixed-light tier 1 license fee by 365 days in a year.

Calculation formula

$25,970.00 annual medium mixed-light tier 1 license fee, divided by 365 days = $71.15 prorated daily license fee.

Calculation formula

$25,970.00 annual medium mixed-light tier 1 license fee, divided by 365 days = $71.15 prorated daily license fee.

Calculation formula – $25,970.00 annual medium mixed-light tier 1 license fee, divided by 365 days = $71.15 prorated daily license fee.

Calculate the number of days remaining in the original license term, by counting the number of days between the date of the request, May 1, 2024, and the original expiration date, October 1, 2024.

There are 153 days remaining in the original license term.

Calculate the amount of credit the license is eligible for by multiplying the number of days remaining in the original license term by the prorated daily license fee.

Calculation formula – 153 (Step 2) x $71.15 (Step 1) = $10,885.95 credit

To calculate fees due to limit operations 

Follow Steps 1 – 3 to calculate the change of expiration date and then follow the bullets below

Calculate the number of days in the modified license term, by counting the number of days between the date of the request, May 1, 2024, and the new expiration date on February 1, 2025.

There are 276 days in the modified license term.

Calculate the prorated daily license fee of the limited operations status by multiplying the annual license fee for a medium mixed-light tier 1 license by 20%, then dividing by 365 days in a year.

Calculation formula – $25,970.00 medium mixed-light tier 1 license fee x 20% limited operations status = $5,194.00. Next divide $5194.00 by 365 days = $14.23 prorated daily license fee for limited operations status.

Calculate the modified license fee by multiplying the number of days in the modified license term, by the prorated daily license fee for the limited operations status.

Calculation formula – 276 (Step 4) x $14.23 (Step 5) = $3,927.48 modified license fee

Calculate the amount due, by subtracting the credit calculated in step 3 from the modified license fee calculated in Step 6.

Calculation formula – $3,927.28 (Step 6) – $10,885.95 (Step 3) = ($6,958.47) in remaining credit that will be refunded by the DCC.

To calculate fees due to reduce the size of the cultivation license 

Follow Steps 1 – 3 to calculate the change of expiration date and then follow the bullets below:

After completing Steps 1 – 3, calculate the number of days in the modified license term, by counting the number of days between the date of the request, May 1, 2024, and the new expiration date on February 1, 2025.
There are 276 days in the modified license term.

Calculate the prorated daily license fee for the reduced-size license by dividing the annual license fee for a small mixed-light tier 1, by 365 days in a year.

Calculation formula – $11,800.00 annual small mixed-light tier 1 license fee, divided by 365 days = $32.33 prorated daily license fee for the reduced-size cultivation license.

Calculate the modified license fee by multiplying the number of days in the modified license term by the prorated daily license fee for the reduced-size cultivation license.

Calculation formula – 276 (Step 4) x $32.33 (Step 5) = $8.923.08 modified license fee.

Calculate the amount due, by subtracting the credit calculated in Step 3 from the modified license fee calculated in Step 6.

Calculation formula – $8923.08 (Step 6) – $10,885.95 (Step 3) = ($1,962.87) in remaining credit that will be refunded by the DCC.
