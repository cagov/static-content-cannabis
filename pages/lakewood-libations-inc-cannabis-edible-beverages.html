Recall overview

Lakewood Libations, Inc., issued a voluntary recall for select products due to a potential packaging defect.

Batch information

Note: The batch number can be located on the COA stamp on the bottom of every bottle.

What to do if you purchased these products

  • Check your package for the UID and batch number above.   
  • If the numbers match: dispose of the product or return to the retailer for proper disposal.   
  • If you are experiencing symptoms or any adverse reactions, contact your physician immediately.  

What licensees need to know 

DCC and/or the Distributor sends email notices to all licensees with a recalled product in their inventory with instructions. If you have questions, or need additional information, please contact recalls@cannabis.ca.gov
