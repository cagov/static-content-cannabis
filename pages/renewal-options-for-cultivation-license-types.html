The Department has adopted emergency regulations to implement Senate Bill 833. Under these regulations, there are new options available for licensed cultivators renewing their license.

Eligibility:

  • Your cultivation license expires on or after March 1, 2024
  • Changes are requested during your renewal period

These opportunities are voluntary and are selected at the discretion of the cultivator. Select an opportunity to learn more.

Place the License in Limited Operations Status
Reduce the Canopy Size of the License Type
Make a One-Time Change to the Current Renewal Date

Place a cultivation license in limited operations status

Selecting this option allows cultivators the opportunity to temporarily pause cultivation commercial cannabis activity, including cultivation on their canopy, at a reduced cost until the next renewal.

Reduce the canopy size of the license type

Make a one-time change to the renewal date

How to request these options 

  1. Review the regulations and the licensing system renewal guidance document.
  2. Login to your CLS account.
  3. Select the license you want to renew.
  4. Input your desired selections and any requested information.
  5. Upload required documentation, if applicable.
  6. Pay the license fee.
  7. Submit your renewal request.
