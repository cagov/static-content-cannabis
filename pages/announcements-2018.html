• Results of Cannabis Banking Feasibility Study to be Released at Public Hearing

    California’s 18-member Cannabis Banking Working Group (CBWG), chaired by State Treasurer John Chiang, will hold a public hearing Thursday, December 27, where they will be presented with the results of the cannabis banking feasibility study prepared by Level 4 Venture.

  • California Department of Tax and Fee Administration – Adoption of Proposed Emergency Cannabis Tax Regulation 3702

    The California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA) has adopted proposed emergency Cannabis Tax Regulation 3702, California Cannabis Track-and-Trace, to require the wholesale cost and retail selling price of cannabis and cannabis products to be recorded in the California Cannabis Track-and-Trace (CCTT) system.

  • Bureau of Cannabis Control – Testing Requirements for Cannabis Goods Reminder

    The Bureau has been receiving questions about which cannabis goods are subject to the phase three testing requirements contained in section 5715 of the Bureau’s regulations.

  • State Cannabis Licensing Authorities Release Proposed Cannabis Regulations Submitted to OAL

    California’s three state cannabis licensing authorities recently announced the release of the proposed cannabis regulations currently under review by the California Office of Administrative Law (OAL).

  • GO-Biz Concludes Community Workshop Series on California Community Reinvestment Grants Program

    Dear California Community Reinvestment Grants Program Interested Parties, The Governor’s Office of Business and Economic Development (GO-Biz) has concluded an initial round of webinars and community workshops across the state to discuss the California Community Reinvestment Grants Program (CalCRG).

  • Bureau of Cannabis Control – Announcement Regarding Temporary License Applications

    While there is no official deadline for submitting a temporary license application to the Bureau of Cannabis Control, the Bureau cannot issue temporary licenses after December 31, 2018.

  • CDFW, SWRCB, and CalCannabis to Host Three Additional Cannabis Cultivation Permitting Enrollment Workshops

    State Water Resource Control Boards (SWRCB), California Department of Fish and Wildlife (CDFW), and the California Department of Food and Agriculture’s CalCannabis Cultivation Licensing division (CalCannabis) will be hosting three Cannabis Cultivation Permitting Enrollment workshops on December 4-6 from 10:00 a.m.

  • Cannabis Cultivation Informational Workshop and Permitting Event

    State Water Board staff will be hosting an Informational Workshop on the proposed updates to the Cannabis Cultivation Policy and Cannabis Cultivation General Order on November 8, 2018 from 4:00 p.m.

  • California Department of Public Health: Sunset of Temporary Licenses

    The Medicinal and Adult-Use Cannabis Regulations and Safety Act (MAUCRSA) contains a sunset clause for temporary license applications (BPC section 26050.1).

  • Deadline for Temporary Cannabis Cultivation License Applications

    Hello CalCannabis Cultivation Licensing Stakeholders, Due to the large number of applications being submitted for temporary cannabis cultivation licenses, the California Department of Food and Agriculture (CDFA) hereby notifies prospective applicants that any application for a temporary license received after December 1, 2018, may NOT be processed in time for us to issue a temporary license before January 1, 2019.
