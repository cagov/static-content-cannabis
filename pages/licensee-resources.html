The Department is committed to providing you with resources to successfully maintain and renew your license. Please review the resources available for each license type.

The documents on this page were created prior to the creation of the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC). Some of them still have logos or contact information for the three former state cannabis programs. We intend to update the documents with DCC information in the near future. You can still use them as resources until we do.
