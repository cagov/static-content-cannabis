The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) operates offices in Northern, Central, and Southern California. At these locations, you can make cash, money order, cashier’s check or check payments, submit license and renewal fees, or make fine and/or cost recovery payments. Appointments are strongly recommended to ensure timely assistance.

General office hours and services

  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m., excluding state holidays
  • Services available: Make license or renewal payments, fine and/or cost recovery payments.

How to schedule an appointment

  1. Request an appointment: Email payments@cannabis.ca.gov or call the office directly (see above).
  2. DCC will send you an email. Respond to the email with the requested information and your preferred date/time.
  3. Receive confirmation: Both the customer and license owner(s) will receive a confirmation email — required before visiting.

Appointment tips

  • Special cases: Cash collection appointments on Friday afternoons are by special request (at Eureka and North Hollywood offices only).
  • Missed appointments: Customers must reschedule if an appointment is missed.

Payment tips

  • Complete online renewals first: Submit renewals online before your appointment.
  • Correct payee: Make checks, money orders, or cashier’s checks payable to “DCC” or “California Department of Cannabis Control”.
  • Include details: Note what the payment is for (e.g., license renewal, application) and include business information. Also include the application/renewal number, CEQA invoice number or the citation, investigation, or license number on your payment.
  • Use tracking for mailed payments: Strongly recommended for checks, money orders, and cashier’s checks.
  • No wire transfers: DCC does not accept wire payments.

Appointment policies

  • Confirmation required: You must receive a confirmation email before coming to your appointment.
  • Cancellations or changes: Contact payments@cannabis.ca.gov or call 1-844-61-CA-DCC (1-844-612-2322).
  • Drop-ins: Limited availability before 4 p.m. (Mon–Thu in Eureka and North Hollywood; Mon–Wed in Rancho Cordova). Not guaranteed — appointments are best.
  • Late arrivals: More than 10 minutes late may require rescheduling, depending on availability.

Payment questions

For all payment related questions, a response will be provided within 48 hours of the request. If we are unable to respond within the 48-hour timeframe, we will contact you concerning the delay.
