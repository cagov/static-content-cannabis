Mission

Created in 2022 by Governor Gavin Newsom, the Unified Cannabis Enforcement Taskforce has been charged by the Governor to better align state efforts and increase cannabis enforcement coordination between state, local and federal partners.

Taskforce leadership

The taskforce is co-chaired by the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) and the California Department of Fish and Wildlife (CDFW) and coordinated by the Homeland Security Division of Cal OES.

Participating agencies

The taskforce includes more than two dozen local, state, and federal partners working together to disrupt the illegal cannabis market.
