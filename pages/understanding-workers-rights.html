As a Cannabis business owner, it’s important to have a general understanding of the laws and best practices related to your role as an employer. The resources on this page outline some areas you should be aware of when it comes to worker’s rights.

Labor

Labor Peace Agreements

State law requires many commercial cannabis licensees to enter into, and abide by, labor peace agreements. Check out our labor peace agreement FAQ.

Labor violations or wage theft

Experiencing labor violations or wage theft? File a complaint with the Department of Industrial Relations.

Safety

Cannabis industry health and safety

CAL/OSHA (The Division of Occupational Safety and Health) provides detailed information about what is expected from employers when it comes to the health and safety of their employees.

Housing

Employee housing resources

Licensees may provide housing for employees during growing and harvest seasons. These employees are entitled to housing that complies with the Housing and Consumer Development (HCD) guidelines.  Learn the legal requirements for employee housing on the Housing and Community Development Webpage.

Housing complaints

Housing complaints can be filed through the HCD Complaint Portal under the section called “Employee Housing Facility”.
