To begin, log in to your account on the Department’s licensing portal.

Access the portal

Type in your Username or Email address and Password then click “Sign In”.

You can renew your license no earlier than 60 calendar days before the expiration of the license or up to 30 calendar days after the license expires.

Next, click on the “My Records” page and locate your manufacturing license then click on “Renew Application.” You can also click on the “Record Number” link.

Be sure you renew before your expiration date, shown clearly in the “Expires On” column. On the day the license expires, if you haven’t yet renewed, all contacts will receive an email stating that you can still renew after paying a late fee penalty which is 50% of your renewal fee.
