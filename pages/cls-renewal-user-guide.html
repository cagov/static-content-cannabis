    The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) adopted regulations in CCR, title 4, sections 15020.1, 15020.2, 15020.3 to implement Senate Bill 833.  Under the regulations, eligible, active cultivation licensees can make certain changes to their license. Learn more about making these changes during renewal through the licensing system.
